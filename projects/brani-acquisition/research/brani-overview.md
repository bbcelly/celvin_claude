## Products

### Brani Balič (flagship)
Warehouse expedition and order fulfillment management tool.

Features:
- Three packing modes: single-order, combined picking+packing, separated picking/packing
- Barcode scanner support (EAN)
- Warehouse position-based picking route optimization
- Multi-package orders with individual label generation
- Automatic carrier pickup scheduling
- Photo documentation of packed orders
- Staff performance metrics and KPI tracking
- Multi-shop support

### Brani Sklad
Warehouse management system.

Features:
- Inventory receipts and shipments
- Batch/expiry tracking
- Intelligent restocking
- Inventory auditing

## Pricing Model

Credit-based, volume-tiered:
- ~1.73 EUR/credit at low volume → ~0.80 EUR/credit at high volume
- Example: 500 orders/month ≈ 12,456 CZK/month
- No setup or installation fee
