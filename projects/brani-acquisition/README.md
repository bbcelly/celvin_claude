## What is Brani?

Brani s.r.o. is a Czech software company (Brno) building warehouse expedition and WMS tools for ecommerce shops. Their flagship product, **Brani Balič**, helps merchants manage order packing, picking routes, carrier handoffs, and warehouse logistics.

- Founded: 2015
- Team: ~9-11 people
- Founder/CEO: Branislav Blaškovič
- Clients: 400+ ecommerce shops
- Bootstrapped — no known external funding

## Why Relevant to Shoptet

Brani Balič is a Shoptet addon partner, listed on both CZ and SK marketplaces. It's a fulfillment-layer tool that sits on top of Shoptet — helping merchants run their physical warehouse operations. It also integrates with Upgates and Abra Flexi.

Acquiring Brani would:
- Bring warehouse/fulfillment capabilities in-house
- Strengthen Shoptet's position in the "Fulfill" part of the merchant journey
- Add ~400 active clients to the platform
- Potentially expand the offering for Shoptet Neo/new markets
