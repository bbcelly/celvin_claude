# Petr Lamač — 30/60/90/120 Day Plan
**Role:** Product Lead, Convert
**Start:** March 2026
**Manager:** Pavel Cvetler
**Team:** Denis Chripák (HoE), Alexandr Borecký (Design Lead)

---

## Day 30 — Learn
*Orient, listen, map. No big moves yet.*

**People**
- 1:1 with Pavel — expectations, communication style, how decisions get made
- Meet Convert trio: Denis Chripák, Alexandr Borecký
- Meet leads and key people across all four Convert product areas
- Intro calls with stakeholders from other superdomains (especially dependencies)

**Product areas — initial mapping**
- Sofa (4th gen templates): current state, release plan, risks for end-of-year target
- 3G templates + DevRel: roadmap status, partner/community obligations
- FE Admin: what's in flight, who owns what
- Mobi App for Admin: current state, team size, backlog health

**Process**
- Get access to all tools: Asana, Slab, Slack, analytics, any internal dashboards
- Understand how Convert currently runs planning and prioritization
- Read existing roadmaps, backlogs, and open decisions

**Success criteria**
- Can describe the current state of each product area without help
- Has a working relationship with Denis and Alexandr
- Knows what's on fire and what's at risk

---

## Day 60 — Contribute
*Take ownership of day-to-day. Start shaping, not just observing.*

**Ownership**
- Running Convert planning and weekly syncs independently
- Managing Convert backlog in Asana — prioritized, up to date
- First pass at a Convert roadmap with Denis and Alexandr aligned

**Sofa**
- Deep-dive into end-of-year release plan — validate timeline, surface blockers
- Agree with Denis on what "on track" looks like and how to track it

**3G templates + DevRel**
- Understand what DevRel commitments exist and whether they're resourced
- Flag any partner or external dependencies

**FE Admin + Mobi App**
- Own active Asana projects end-to-end
- Identify any resource or prioritization conflicts across the four areas

**Success criteria**
- Pavel is no longer proxying Convert decisions
- Petr is the go-to person for Convert questions from other teams
- Sofa risk picture is clear and documented

---

## Day 90 — Lead
*Drive the roadmap. Operate with full autonomy.*

**Strategy**
- Convert roadmap for H2 2026 drafted and shared with Pavel
- Sofa release plan locked — milestones, go/no-go criteria, contingency
- Priorities across all four areas reconciled and visible

**Team**
- Regular rhythm with Denis and Alexandr working well
- Any team process gaps identified and being addressed
- Escalations to Pavel are edge cases, not routine

**Cross-domain**
- Convert dependencies with other superdomains mapped and managed
- Active in cross-superdomain planning (if it exists)

**Success criteria**
- H2 roadmap exists and Pavel has reviewed it
- Sofa has a credible plan to hit end-of-year
- Petr can represent Convert at leadership level without prep

---

## Day 120 — Deliver
*Measurable impact. Strategic contributor.*

**Outcomes**
- Sofa: clear milestone hit or concrete plan to recover if behind
- Convert backlog: healthy, prioritized, with clear quarterly focus
- DevRel: any commitments for 3G templates are tracked and resourced
- FE Admin + Mobi App: at least one meaningful improvement shipped or in review

**Strategic**
- Petr has a point of view on Convert's biggest opportunities for 2026–2027
- Brings proposals to Pavel, not just status updates
- Actively contributing to department-level discussions

**Success criteria**
- Pavel trusts Petr to run Convert end-to-end
- Sofa is on track or risk is managed with a plan
- No open ownership gaps in Convert

## Check-in Cadence
- Weekly 1:1 with Pavel (first 60 days: focus on blockers and orientation)
- Day 30 review: shared assessment of first month
- Day 60 review: roadmap draft reviewed together
- Day 90 review: H2 plan aligned
- Day 120 review: outcomes check, adjust expectations for rest of year
