# Shoptet

Shoptet is a SaaS ecommerce platform founded in 2008, serving 45,000+ eshops. It holds roughly one-third of the ecommerce market in Czech Republic and Slovakia.

## Products & Revenue Streams
- **Core platform** — Subscription-based ecommerce SaaS (~$15/month, freemium tier available)
- **Extensions marketplace** — Third-party integrations and add-ons
- **Shoptet Premium** — Enterprise/custom stores
- **Shoptet Neo** — Expansion product being rolled out to new markets via team.blue brands

## Markets
- Primary: Czech Republic, Slovakia, Hungary
- Expanding: Other European markets via team.blue

## Ownership
Shoptet was acquired by team.blue. Integration is ongoing.
